1. contoh email formal sama informal dalam bahasa Inggris?​


Contoh Email Formal

Dear Sir or Madam,

I am interested in Clear Lake University's International Student Exchange program. I am a third-year student from Woodcreek University, taking up my Bachelor's in History and I wish to acquire more experiences by attending a foreign university for a semester.

I am a hard-working student in the Social Sciences Department here at Woodcreek, and after graduation, I intend to continue my studies in this field in Greece.

I would like to request any application materials, including information about financial aid for foreign undergraduate students. Please use the above address. Thank you in advance.

Sincerely,

Abigail Olsen

Contoh Email Informal

Dear Adam,

How are you? I hope you're doing well.

Sorry I haven't been in touch for such a long time but I've been really busy balancing both a part-time job and my university applications. I've heard that you've just recently graduated from high school. Congratulations! I wish I was there during the celebration, it must've been great.

Anyways, I'd love to hear all your news and I'm hoping we can get together soon to catch up. I'll be visiting town soon to check up on my granddad, maybe we could meet up then?

Looking forward to hearing from you!

Best Wishes,

Rose

8. formal email inggris dari hi, how are you?


Jawaban:

Hi :

• Good (Morning/Afternoon/Evening)

• Dear Mr, Mrs, Ms,

How are you :

• How have you been up to?

• How do you do

Penjelasan:

• Good (Morning/Afternoon/Evening) : These are formal ways of saying “hello”, which change depending on the time of day. 

• Dear Mr, Mrs, Ms : Always open a formal email with salutation. Address the recipient by name is preferred and include the person's title

• How have you been up to : This greeting question is only asked by people who have already made contact. If someone asks you “how have you been?” they want to know if you have been well since the last time the two of you made contact

• How do you do : This greeting is VERY formal, and quite uncommon, but it may still be used by some older people

31. laporan penelitian yang bersifat non formal


Laporan nonformal merupakan laporan yang ditulis secara populer, dimana menggunakan kata- kata sederhana, terkadang juga diselingi dengan humor. Laporan yang tidak memenuhi beberapa unsur formal. Laporan nonformal bersifat pribadi yang disesuaikan dengan kepentingan penulisannya

Penjelasan

Hallo teman- teman BrainlyLovers!!! pada waktu luang kali ini kita bersama- sama akan  mencoba  menjawab pertanyaan yang berhubungan dengan pelajaran sekolah menengah atas mata pelajaran sosiologi. Semangat belajar yaa teman- teman semoga sukses!!!

Pengertian laporan:

Laporan merupakan suatu wahana penyampaian informasi, berita, pengetahuan atau gagasan dari seseorang yang ditujukan kepada orang lain.

Dasar membuat laporan:

1.Kegiatan menulis laporan ilmiah adalah kegiatan utama terakhir dari sebuah kegiatan ilmiah

2.Laporan ilmiah memuat permasalahan yang ditulis secara jelas, benar, ringkas, dan terperinci

3.Laporan ilmiah adalah media yang bagus intuk berkomunikasi dalam lingkungan akademis ataupun sesama ilmuwan

4.Laporan ilmiah adalah suatu dokumen mengenai kegiatan ilmiah guna memecahkan masalah secara jujur, jelas, dan juga tepat prosedur, hasil, alat serta implikasinya

5.Laporan ilmiah bisa digunakan sebagai acuan para ilmuwan lain sehingga syarat- syarat dari tulisan ilmiah berlaku juga untuk laporan

Fungsi laporan:

1.Alat pendokumentasian data

2.Alat pertanggungjawaban secara tertulis

3.Alat pengambilan keputusan

4.Alat studi banding, dan

5.Melatih berpikir sistematis

Ciri- ciri laporan:

1.Obyektif

2.Bahasa baku

3.Sistematis

4.Dibuat atas permintaan

5.Pembacanya tertentu
