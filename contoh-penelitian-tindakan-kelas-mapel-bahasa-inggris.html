Jawaban:

congratulation

noun

1. The act of expressing joy or acknowledgment, as for the achievement or good fortune of another.

2. often congratulations An expression of such joy or acknowledgment: sent him my congratulations on winning the award.

interjection

congratulations

Used to express such joy or acknowledgment: I heard you got a promotion. Congratulations!

Origin

From Middle French, con- +‎ gratulation, cognate with Latin congratulor (congrātulor, “I congratulate”).

15. Apa itu passive voice?berikan contohnya !Ini sebenarnya bukan pelajaran kelas 7 kak,tapi saya penasaran karena di Brainly banyak orang menanyakan ini...Mapel : Bahasa Inggrismaaf salah mapel


passive voice dipelajari nanti di kelas 9 dan SMA.

kalimat pasif itu digunakan untuk mengistimewakan subject. misalnya:

I was born (saya dilahirkan)

My homework was done by me (pr ku dikerjakan olehku)

Apa itu passive?

Bentuk pasif adalah bentuk verba yang memiliki bentuk yang sama dengan bentuk medial, kecuali dalam kala akan datang dan kala aoris. Dalam bentuk pasif tindakan dilakukan ke atas subjek. Jadi, subjeklah yang menerima tindakan dari objek.

Contoh kalimat passive past tense:

Active: We cleaned our house yesterday.

=> Kami membersihkan rumah kami kemarin.

Passive: Our house was cleaned by us yesterday.

=> Rumah kami dibersihkan oleh kami kemarin

Rumus passive:

S (direct object) + is/am/are + past participle +/- by … (agent)

Dalam passive,ada juga active. Jadi, active itu seperti kebalikannya passive.

16. ✨Quiz ✨Mapel : Bahasa Inggris Kelas : 9 Semester : 1 ​


Jawaban opsi A, B dan C di link sebelumnya https://brainly.co.id/tugas/43494578

(d) many recycled handcrafts are produced by the woman at her house.(e) many losses of lives and properties were caused by tornado last night.

Pembahasan

Perintah soal: Change the sentences into passive voice.

➡️ Ubah kalimat menjadi kalimat pasif.

Berikut berdasarkan rumus

A. Present Tense

Active (+) S + V1 + O

The woman produced many recycled handcrafts at her house.

Passive (+) O + Be (is/am/are) + V3 + By S

many recycled handcrafts are produced by the woman at her house.

B. Past Tense

Active (+) S + V2 + O

Last night tornado caused many losses of lives and properties

Passive (+) O + Be (was/were) + V3 + By S

many losses of lives and properties were caused by tornado last night.
